About Sprout & Grow

Our Story

Sprout & Grow began as a simple backyard project, ust a handful of seedlings, a borrowed potting bench, and a desire to help neighbors grow healthy, vibrant gardens. What started as weekend workshops and friendly plant swaps has since blossomed into Raleigh's trusted local source for organic plants and gardening support.

While we've grown over the years, our heart hasn't changed. We're still here to inspire curiosity, share knowledge, and help every gardener—no matter their space or experience level—discover the joy of watching something thrive under their care.

Our Mission

At Sprout & Grow, we believe that gardening reconnects us to the things that matter. Nature, community, and the simple joy of nurturing something with your own hands.

Our mission is to make organic gardening accessible, sustainable, and deeply rewarding. We're committed to offering high-quality plants, eco-friendly products, and hands-on guidance so every gardener feels confident and supported. Whether you're tending a backyard plot or a sunny apartment windowsill, we're here to help you grow with purpose.

Our Values

Sustainability
- We're committed to organic growing methods, eco-friendly products, and practices that protect the health of our community and our environment.
Community
- From workshops and seasonal events to neighborhood partnerships, we believe gardening is a powerful way to bring people together.
Education
- We offer resources, classes, and personalized support to make gardening simple, approachable, and fun for all ages.
Care
- Every plant and every customer matters. Whether you're nurturing a single herb or building a full garden, we're here to help every step of the way.

Why Choose Us

We take pride in offering organic, hardy, and thoughtfully curated plants that thrive in the Raleigh climate. Our team of experienced gardeners is here to guide you toward selections that fit your space, goals, and growing style.

With locally grown plants, sustainable tools, and a welcoming community of fellow gardeners, Sprout & Grow is more than a nursery. We're your partner in creating a healthier, greener future.
